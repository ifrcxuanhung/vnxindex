PSI - IFRC CONFERENCE

The “Introduction of PVN-Indexes and other attractive investable indexes for Vietnam stock market’ Conference, was successfully held on March 25th with participation of many speakers coming from Viet Nam principal stock market members: State Securities Commission, Ha Noi stock market (HNX), Ho Chi Minh stock market (HSX), Vietfund - VFM, Petrol Securities Incoporate - PSI - and Index provider – IFRC - and others participants.

Legal and technical aspects are in preparation phrase to start ETFs activities in the second quarter of this year and in 2016 for Vietnam’s derivatives market, according to Dr. Nguyễn Sơn, head of market development department under the State Securities Commission. The project of merging the two stocks was also raised in order to facilitate the market. The two stock markets are finishing rules and trading mechanism for these new products. VFM showed their enthusiasm and their interest in developing and presenting ETFs to investors as soon as all above preparation steps are accomplished.   

PSI, the manager and operator of PVN-Index, and IFRC, France-based Intelligent Financial Research & Consulting (IFRC), index provider, expressed ambition from their cooperation to develop many indexes for the whole Vietnamese market, serving underlying for derivative product in close future. At the occasion, IFRC founder and CEO Dr. Mai Huu Minh presented their VNX family that covers 400 indices (www.vnxindex.com), including Benchmark, Size, Sectors, Blue Chips, Strategy and Theme indexes. These provide full and transparent information on indicators that followed the standards of international investment and are potential underlying of funds and structured products.

VNX PROVINCIAL INDEXES

The Vietnam Geographical Economic Indexes At the national level, Vietnam is trying to improve the competitiveness and international integration. Within the scope of local, regional leaders (province, city) were also eager to reform, to develop policies and strategies for local economic development.

The competition is silent, drastic and effective but has not been evaluated completely. PAPI (The Viet Nam Provincial Governance and Public Administration Performance Index) and PCI (Provincial Competitiveness Index) biased toward measuring policy effectiveness. Therefore, the Intelligent Financial Research & Consulting (IFRC ) initiative to create a Vietnam geographical economic index measuring the economic efficiency of the provinces, cities and regions in Vietnam

The Vietnam geographical economic indexes are designed and calculated based on the data of companies listed on stock exchanges, has its head office located in that geographic area. These indexes are calculated in proportion to market capitalization, in the price and total return version; and conversion of different currencies (VND, USD, EUR, JPY).

The Vietnam Geographical Economic Indexes series are including :

  • The Provincial Indexes (actually 25 biggest provinces and cities, only provinces having at least 5 listed companies are calculated)

  • The Regional Indexes (6 regions).

New website:  www.provincial.vnxindex.com

VIETNAM VIRTUAL DERIVATIVES MARKET (VNVDM)

Vietnam has announced that derivatives market will be launched in 2016. As experience in global financial markets, achievement of successful operation in that market requires much preparation in advance, even in a long time before launching this sophisticated market.  

On this special occasion, We, Intelligent Financial Research & Consulting (IFRC), are proud to initiate the project of the creation of  a new, state of the art Virtual Derivatives Market.

Our goal is to create the Vietnam Virtual Derivatives Market (VNVDM) that serves as an online trading platform which simulates a real trading environment and allows participants a risk-free opportunity to learn and trade derivatives on equities, bonds, or commodities, as well as test their trading knowledge and market savvy.

New website: www.vnvdm.com
