First concrete cooperation: Women CEO project

The Women CEO Project is the first step of a larger project on the relationship between corporate performance and governance. Other topics will be the presence of women on the boards, capital held by the state, capital held by foreign investors, or family businesses . This will be achieved using two approaches: The first is academic, through empirical researches, publications in financial journals verifying the existence of this relationship, seeking the influence of factors such as the development (frontiers, emerging or developed markets), culture, religion, education… of the countries in question. The second is financial by creating a financial portfolio strategy as measured by an index into the final objective to propose investable products as funds or structured products. The index thus constructed will be somehow a continuous measurement of the performance if any. In the second step, if the data are available, we extend this research to private equities.

VNX Logistics Indexes

Under World Trade Organization (WTO) commitments, Vietnam will open logistics market in 2014 to foreign investors. Vietnamese logistics companies have to improve their information transparency. The VNX Logistics indexes can help it, measure the performance of these companies, and offer to investors’ tools to make decision or to invest in this fast growing sector. VNX Logistics indexes Family The VNX Logistics indexes family is composed by 3 indexes: VNX Logistics index The VNX Logistics index is a full market capitalisation index, and composed Logistics companies trading on the HSX and HNX stock market exchanges. VNX Logistics Equal Weighted index This index is the equal-weight version of the VNX Logistics index. The index includes the same constituents as the capitalization weighted VNX Logistics index, but each company is allocated a fixed and equal weight of the index total at each semester rebalance. VNX Top 10 Logistics index The index is comprised of 10 leading Logistics companies trading on the HSX and HNX stock market exchanges. It is designed to provide investors with tradable exposure to the Logistics sector. Their selection is based on the 12-month average daily value traded and the free float capitalisation. No stock has a weight of more than 15% at the rebalancing.

VLI and IFRC join forces to develop the VLI Logistics indexes

VLI, Research and Development Institute of Logistics, in collaboration with IFRC Vietnam has successfully created Vietnam Logistics Indexes. These indexes provide all information about the Logistics industry, and become the standard for the industry in Vietnam and the basis for financial investment. The index meet six criteria: feasibility, representation, continuity, independence, transparency and usefulness. VLI Logistics indexes Family: VLI Logistics index VLI Logistics Equal Weighted index VLI Top 10 Logistics index These indexes are calculated in Price and Total return versions and converted into 4 different major currencies: VND, USD, EUR and JPY.
