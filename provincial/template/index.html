The Petrovietnam Securities Incorporated (PSI) and Intelligent Financial Research & Consulting Viet Nam (IFRC Viet Nam) have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) to pursue a continuing strategic cooperation regarding the potential development of index products and services in Viet Nam.

Under the terms of the MOU, PSI and IFRC will join their force and collaborate in market research activities in order the build and develop index products Viet Nam stock market( HSX and HNX market). Further cooperation will cover also Size, Sector, Group Indexes to supply more information channels for investors about this dynamic emerging market, thereby bulding images of PSI and IFRC and confirm them as the first index provider in the country.

In this framework of cooperation, the "Introduction of PVN-Indexes and other attractive investable indexes for Viet Nam stock market" Conference, jointly organized by Intelligent Financial Research & Consulting (IFRC), PetroVietnam (PVN) and PetroVietnam Secutities Incorporated (PSI), will take place January 16, 2014, in the Majestic Hotel (1 Dong Khoi Street, District 1, Ho chi Minh City, VIETNAM) from 13:30 pm.
